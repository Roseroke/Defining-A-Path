Defining a Path

A comprehensive and practical attempt to address and solve the social and economic problems of our time, proposing tangible solutions for work, money, and purpose.

Redefining the Role of Government

The core idea is to redefine government as an entity entrusted with the population's wellbeing, grounded in the philosophy of **Collective Servitism**â€”where value is measured by service to others, not self-enrichment.

This involves streamlining agencies and de-commodifying essentials like healthcare and housing. The government's role is not to control, but to empower citizens by providing a baseline of support and competing with private enterprise to drive innovation and fairness.

Core Principles

  • Ensure the wellbeing of the population.
  • Remain accountable to the Rule of Law.
  • Provide employment & public services.
  • Incentivize selflessness over self-enrichment.
  • Diminish exploitation while strengthening liberty.

A New Framework for Industry

To create a more equitable society, a new framework of government-run, publicly-accountable agencies is proposed. These are designed to address critical societal needs and provide meaningful work for all who opt-in.

CHRA

Creates personalized "Stability Plans" based on objective needs, connecting citizens to work, education, and housing.

AIREA

Spearheads ethical AI development and manages sustainable energy resources for the collective benefit.

DORH

Allocates vacant housing and develops initiatives for habitability, ensuring shelter for all system participants.

SERA & WMA

Manages waste, declutters environmentally affected zones, and provides critical disaster relief.

HST & RASA

Coordinates national supply lines and manages the prospecting and allocation of raw materials.

HMS & DOE

Provides universal healthcare and develops a relevant, modern curriculum for the youth.

The Universal Catalogue (UC)

The UC is the engine of the new economy. By performing societally beneficial tasks, citizens earn "Credits" to acquire goods and services, from daily necessities to housing. This directly links contribution to quality of life.

1

Complete Contracts

Opt-in and complete tasks offered by local Embassies, from environmental cleanup to infrastructure maintenance.

2

Earn Credits

Upon task completion, credits tied to the real-world capacity of supply warehouses are awarded to a secure digital wallet.

3

Acquire Goods

Use Credits to "purchase" items from the curated catalogue, ensuring production meets need, not just profit.

Accountability & Safeguards

On Authoritarianism

This proposal is for a constitutional democracy where the Rule of Law is paramount. To prevent abuse of power, key safeguards are built directly into the system's foundation.

The Right to Appeal

Any decision by a CHRA case manager denying a citizen access to housing, work, or credits can be formally appealed, ensuring due process.

Citizens' Oversight Board

Each region will have an independent board of legal professionals and residents to review appeals. This board has the authority to overturn unfair decisions and sanction administrators, ensuring power remains with the people.
